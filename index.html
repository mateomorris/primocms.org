Drag-n-drop page building

Build your site's pages by dragging and dropping your blocks onto the page, unincumbered by tedious design options.

Visual content editing

Update your text, images, and links directly on the page & manage everything else from the Fields view.

Access your blocks' code with a click - right from the browser. Each block is a Svelte component that can do anything.
